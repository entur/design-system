### MediaCard

Alltid `vertical` ved mobil

- as: `a`| `button` | `React.ElementType`
  - default: `a`
- size: `small` | `medium`
  - default: `medium`
- `vertical`: `boolean`
  - default: `false`
- variant: `illustration`| `image`
  - default `image`
